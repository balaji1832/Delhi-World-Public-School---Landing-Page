About VELS
Global School

A forward-thinking institution dedicated to shaping confident, compassionate, and future-ready learners since 2017.

VELS Global School, Ludhiana is a forward-thinking educational institution dedicated to shaping confident, compassionate, and future-ready learners. Established in 2017, the school was founded with a vision to provide world-class education that combines strong academic foundations with holistic development.

At VELS Global School, learning goes far beyond the classroom. Our curriculum, aligned with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), encourages inquiry, creativity, and critical thinking while nurturing the intellectual, emotional, physical, and social growth of every student.

We believe that education must prepare children not only for examinations but also for life. Through innovative teaching practices, technology-enabled classrooms, and experiential learning opportunities, students develop the skills needed to succeed in an increasingly dynamic and interconnected world.

Our vibrant campus provides a stimulating environment where curiosity is encouraged, talents are discovered, and young minds are inspired to dream big.

With a dedicated team of passionate educators, modern infrastructure, well-equipped laboratories, libraries, and activity spaces, we ensure that every learner receives the guidance and opportunities needed to achieve excellence.

At the heart of VELS Global School is the belief that every child has unique potential. By nurturing values such as integrity, empathy, resilience, and responsibility, we strive to develop not only successful students but also thoughtful global citizens who will contribute meaningfully to society.

OUR LEARNING APPROACH

Methodology

The School functions towards imparting Holistic Education which will lead to the development of the students as responsible citizens. We inculcate values in them so that they efficiently discharge their duties as individuals and contribute towards world peace. We engage and empower students with skills and knowledge through engaged learning programs. We make our students custodians of their own, mental, physical and spiritual well-being. We aim to nurture excellent all-rounders. To acquaint students with the complete knowledge and allow them to break and set their own limits is what we aim at. Meaning Education is what the school aims for, education should not be confined to the only assimilation of knowledge, it is the collective effort to create better future with the strength of integration.

Extra curriculum activities in the school inculcate zeal and enthusiasm in the students, and also balances the physical and emotional growth, boosting their stamina. The sportsman spirit prepares the students for further challenges, making them strong both emotionally and physically. Equal opportunities are provided to every student to showcase their talents. We provide them with the proper exposure to unleash their true potential, it is our responsibility to encourage and help them discover their potential.

Our Campus

Facilities

Our campus offers modern facilities designed to support holistic learning and student development. From well-equipped classrooms and laboratories to sports and activity spaces, every facility ensures a safe, engaging, and enriching environment.

Auditorium

The campus includes an ultra modern hi-tech auditorium which has a capacity of over 1000. It provides the perfect platform for students to exhibit their talent. The cinema-styled auditorium is of professional standards and motivates students to perform on a grand stage while accustoming them to face the spotlight with confidence.

Curriculum

The Curriculum of DWPS World Foundation adheres to the National Curriculum Framework 2005 (NCF 2005) and the CBSE curriculum. The Foundation believes that the classroom experiences of children are to be organized in a manner that permits them to construct knowledge in consonance with his/her own nature and context, thus providing equal learning opportunities to all. Teaching aims at enhancing the child’s innate desire and acumen to learn. Activity is at the heart of the child’s attempt to sense the world around. Therefore, every resource is deployed in a child-centric manner to enable them to express, handle objects, explore their natural and social milieu and to grow up in good health.

Transport

The school has its own fleet of new air-conditioned buses to ferry the children to and fro. A teacher travels in every bus and disembarks only after the last child is dropped off safely. Safety on the road is given paramount importance. The students embark and disembark within the school campus under the vigilance of transport managers and teaching staff. An escort teacher and helping staff accompany the children in the bus. All the buses are equipped with a medical kit for emergency. In case of a delay due to unforeseen circumstances, parents are immediately informed through SMS.The school transport will cover almost all areas of Ludhiana as well as the satellite towns within a range of 15 – 20 km.

Library

Our e-library can be accessed by students from within the school as well as from home. This enables the students to not only read their favorite titles in paperback or e-books but also to prepare presentations. Such exercises help them to improve their presentation and communication skills.

Social Science Lab

Social Science Laboratory has equipment, apparatus and materials required for each subject, such as maps, globe, ethnic artifacts, etc. to promote practical learning. It provides a work space for students and teachers for interactive activities such as role playing, visualization, competing/cooperating, problem solving or focussed group discussions.

Computer Lab & Language Lab

The Computer Lab is well resourced with high-speed central servers and the latest educational software (with adequate security and safety features). Every student has access to a terminal in the lab. We are also keen to teach children about personal safety in the use of social media to ensure they are fully aware of the social risks involved. Parents can access the up-to-date progress of their children in each subject via the school’s website.Our Language Laboratory is fitted with latest multimedia equipment and audio-visual aids. Students are able to practice speaking a new language, record their work and take part in interactive sessions to improve their language skills while enjoying the experience.

Mathematics Lab

The Maths Lab allows students to explore mathematical concepts via games, puzzles and other modern teaching aids. In addition to teaching in the lab, students are encouraged to use lab facilities on their own to inculcate in them a spi r it of discovery and inventions.

Science & Robotics Lab

The school has also built a Science and Robotics lab for students to learn the wonders of science and also train them to build and control robots. Fostering scientific thinking and reasoning, the lab helps students to move beyond the textbook and promotes learning-by-doing.
